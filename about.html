Who are We?

The Association of Certified Public Operational Standards (ACPOSL) is a premier organization in Sri Lanka dedicated to promoting the
principles and standards of professional operations. We support and connect certified professionals, enhancing their role in contributing to
the economic and operational development of Sri Lanka. Our
members are leaders in operations management, committed to
excellence, ethics, and growth.
