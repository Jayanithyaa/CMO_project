Know your rights

As a music creator, you have the legal right to control how your work is used, distributed, and
monetized. Whether it’s live performances, streaming, radio airplay, or reproductions, you
deserve fair compensation. Understanding your rights ensures you get paid for your creativity
while protecting your music from unauthorized use.
