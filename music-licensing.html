License Types & Who Needs Them

At ACPOSL, we work to protect and support Sri Lankan music creators by
ensuring fair rights, royalties, and recognition.

Public Performance License

  • Who Needs It? Restaurants, bars, hotels, retail stores, fitness centers, public events.
  • Covers: Music played in public spaces, including background music and live performances.

Online & Digital Licensing

  • Who Needs It? Websites, streaming platforms, content creators, mobile apps.
  • Covers: Digital music distribution, podcasts, and online streaming.

Radio & TV Licensing

  • Who Needs It? Radio stations, TV channels, cable networks.
  • Covers: Broadcast of music on radio and television

Background Music Licensing

  • Who Needs It? Hotels, malls, coffee shops, waiting rooms, salons.
  • Covers: Pre-recorded music played in business environments.

Live Event Licensing

  • Who Needs It? Concert organizers, event planners, corporate events.
  • Covers: Royalties for music performed at live events.

Reproduction License

  • Who Needs It? Record labels, CD/DVD manufacturers, digital distributors.
  • Physical or digital reproduction of copyrighted music.

Business & Commercial Licensing

  • Who Needs It? Advertising agencies, corporate videos, brand campaigns.
  • Covers: Use of music in commercials and promotional content.

Streaming & Digital Platforms Licensing

  • Who Needs It? Streaming services, YouTube creators, gaming platforms.
  • Covers: Online use of copyrighted music for streaming and monetization.
