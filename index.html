About ACPOSL

ACPOSL is committed to protecting the rights of Sri Lankan musicians,
lyricists, composers, and publishers. Our mission is to ensure fair
compensation, advocate for strong copyright protection, and support
the creative community by combating piracy and promoting ethical music
usage. Through policy advocacy, legal support, and industry
collaboration, we empower music creators to thrive in a fair and
sustainable environment.
