What We Do – Protecting & Supporting Music Creators

At ACPOSL, we work to protect and support Sri Lankan music creators by ensuring fair rights, royalties, and recognition.

Advocacy & Public Policy

We work with policymakers to strengthen copyright laws, fight piracy, and protect musicians' rights.

Industry Support & Networking

We offer workshops, training, legal guidance, and opportunities to connect with industry professionals.

International Affiliations

We collaborate with global music rights organizations to ensure Sri Lankan artists receive their royalties worldwide.

Copyright Protection

We help musicians secure their rights and prevent unauthorized use of their work.

Royalty Collection & Distribution

We collect royalties from radio, TV, streaming, and public performances, ensuring creators get paid fairly.

Music Licensing

We provide legal licenses for businesses, events, and media platforms that use music, making sure creators receive proper compensation.

About ACPOSL

ACPOSL is committed to protecting the rights of Sri Lankan musicians, lyricists, composers, and publishers. Our mission is to ensure fair compensation, advocate for strong copyright protection, and support the creative community by combating piracy and promoting ethical music usage. Through policy advocacy, legal support, and industry collaboration, we empower music creators to thrive in a fair and sustainable environment.
